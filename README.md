# Name

## 📜 Essence

Create: Cardboard Things is strange addon that adds 9 cardboard-themed items inspired by the original Cardboard Sword from Create. Each item comes with its own unusual, sometimes surprising behavior. In this mod, cardboard isn’t just a meme — it’s a mechanic.

## 🎯 Features

- Cardboard Elytra – infinite while flying, instantly breaks when you land.
- Cardboard Arrow – deals no direct damage but launches targets across blocks.
- Cardboard Pickaxe – slow and weak, but unbreakable — perfect for farms or "best friend".
- Cardboard Ball – doesn’t knockback, but deals real damage (cardboard’s tougher than it looks).
- Cardboard Rocket – used only for Elytra boost.
- Cardboard Saddle – a frame for a future saddle.
- Cardboard Totem – saves you from death and lets you quickly escape or leap away.
- Cardboard Ingot – ever felt like the Cardboard Sword from Create doesn't knock back hard enough? this ingot upgrades that experience on an anvil.
- Cardboard Shears – mysterious. Why do they exist? Try them and see.
